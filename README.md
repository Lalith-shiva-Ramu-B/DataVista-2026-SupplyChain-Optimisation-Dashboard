# DataVista-2026-SupplyChain-Optimisation-Dashboard
DataVista 2026 Competition: A Power BI solution for logistics reliability, waste mitigation, and inventory synchronization. 

<h2>Exexutive Summary</h2>
<p>This project provides a comprehensive analytical dashboards designed to identify and mitigate operational risks within the supplychain.By analyzing over 100K transcation I found the product and categories which the comapany should focus to reduce the waste costs and increase the profits and revenue. </p>

<h2> Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)</h2> 
<ul>
<li>Total revenue: 591.15M $</li>
<li>Total Profits: 79.78M $</li>
<li>Total Waste cost: 163.22M $</li>
<li>Inventory Opportunity: 1.92M $</li>
<li>Average Lead Time: 38.02 hours</li>
<li>Reliability Index: 19.60</li>
</ul>

<h2>The 4 pillar Analytical Framework</h2>
<h3>Revenue Analysis (Financial Health)</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Identified Pharmaceuticals as Profit Driver ($39.5M)</li>
  <li>Highlights that 84.95% Revenue is Organic ie Non Promoted </li>
  <li>Highlights that 70.9% revenue is made during weekdays </li>
</ul>
<h3>Waste & Operational Analysis (Fragility Index)</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Identified Pharmaceuticals Category has the highest waste cost ($13.6M)</li>
  <li>Identified Forzen Meals has highest waste% 50% but it has less effect on revenue </li>
  <li>Identified Abuse Events 80K is one of causes of waste producted </li>
</ul>
<h3>Inventory & Demand Analysis (Synchronization)</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Manages a $875.91M total inventory value. </li>
  <li>Lost Revenue of $1.92M due to stockouts</li>
  <li>Found Sandwiches, Soups, wraps, Sushi are stockouts Products </li>
</ul>
<h3>Lead Time Analysis (Logistics Reliability)</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Average Supplier Score: 8.21</li>
  <li>Average Handling Score: 7</li>
  <li>Pinpoints Suppliers 11 and 12 as high-lead-time outliers</li>
</ul>

<h3>Conclusion</h3>
<p>To reduce the wast costs, the organisation must focus on the warehouse and the Operations Team, so that Temperature Abuse Events can be reduced, the stockout Units of products such as Sandwiches etc and Handling score can be improved overall. </p>
